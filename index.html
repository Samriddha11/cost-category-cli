Harness Cost Category Manager

Backup, Restore, and Manage Cost Categories with Ease

Problem Statement

Managing and maintaining Harness Cost Categories in an enterprise environment presents several challenges:

  • Complex cost category configurations make onboarding new teams challenging.
  • Any accidental addition or misconfiguration can lead to immediate cost impact, requiring fast rollback.
  • Lack of native version control makes rollback and auditing difficult.
  • Cost categories are dynamic and change as the organization scales, increasing maintenance complexity.
  • Before major structural changes, capturing a snapshot is critical to minimize unexpected cost impact.

Solution

The Harness Cost Category Manager provides an automated way to manage the lifecycle of cost categories with:

  • Full backup of all cost categories, with each category saved as an individual JSON file.
  • Quick restoration of single or multiple cost categories, supporting auto_rename to prevent conflicts.
  • Version-like snapshots for safe rollback before any structural or functional changes.
  • Simple CLI interface for listing, backing up, restoring, and deleting cost categories.
python cost_category_manager.py

# Menu options:
# 1. List Cost Categories
# 2. Backup a Cost Category
# 3. Backup ALL Cost Categories
# 4. Restore a Cost Category from Backup
# 5. Restore ALL Cost Categories (with auto_rename option)
# 6. Delete a Cost Category
# 7. Exit

Benefit to Customer

  • Ensures quick rollback in case of accidental cost category changes.
  • Provides version-like backups to maintain control over evolving configurations.
  • Enables auditing and historical tracking of cost category states.
  • Reduces risk of unexpected cloud cost impact during changes.
  • Simplifies team onboarding and cost management across growing organizations.

Benefit to Harness

  • Enhances customer confidence in Harness CCM by providing a safety net for changes.
  • Reduces support cases related to accidental cost category misconfigurations.
  • Improves enterprise adoption with a practical backup and restore workflow.
  • Positions Harness as a reliable FinOps platform with robust lifecycle management.
